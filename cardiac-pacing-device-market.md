<p><p>The cardiac pacing device market encompasses various types of devices designed to regulate heart rhythm. Temporary cardiac pacing provides short-term support, often in emergency scenarios. Single-chamber cardiac pacing stimulates either the atrium or ventricle, while dual-chamber pacing coordinates both. Single-chamber ICDs focus on preventing sudden cardiac arrests, and dual-chamber ICDs offer both pacing and defibrillation. Three-chamber CRT-P devices help manage heart failure by synchronizing heart contractions, whereas three-chamber CRT-D devices combine this function with defibrillation capabilities for enhanced treatment.</p></p>
