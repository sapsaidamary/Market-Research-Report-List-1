<p><p>The rooftop solar photovoltaic (PV) installation market primarily comprises two types: crystalline silicon PV and thin film PV. Crystalline silicon PV, including monocrystalline and polycrystalline variants, is favored for its high efficiency and longevity but tends to be more expensive. In contrast, thin film PV is lighter, flexible, and generally less costly, making it versatile for various applications, although with lower efficiency. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different consumer needs and installation scenarios in the solar market.</p></p>
