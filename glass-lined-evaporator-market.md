<p><strong>The Glass-lined Evaporator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-Rotor</li><li>P-Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-lined evaporator market is primarily segmented into two types: L-Rotor and P-Rotor. L-Rotor evaporators feature a long, cylindrical design with a horizontal orientation, facilitating effective heat transfer and efficient mixing. They are typically used in applications requiring low to medium viscosity materials. In contrast, P-Rotor evaporators have a more compact, vertical design, offering enhanced efficiency for thicker fluids and better space utilization. Both types are essential in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing, where corrosion resistance and hygienic conditions are crucial.</p></p>
