<p><strong>The Armored Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Tape Armored Cable</li><li>Metal Wire Armored Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Armored cables are designed with protective layers to withstand mechanical stress, environmental factors, and external damage. **Metal Tape Armored Cable (MTA)** uses a thin, flat metal tape wrapped around the cable, offering flexibility and lightweight protection, ideal for indoor or less harsh environments. **Metal Wire Armored Cable (MWA)** employs steel or aluminum wires for robust, heavy-duty shielding, suitable for outdoor, underground, or industrial applications requiring high durability. Both types enhance safety and longevity, with MTA prioritizing flexibility and MWA focusing on strength.</p></p>
