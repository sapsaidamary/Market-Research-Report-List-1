<p><p>The space launch vehicle market is categorized into three main types based on payload capacity: small-lift, medium-lift, and heavy-lift launch vehicles. Small-lift launch vehicles can carry lighter payloads, typically under 2,000 kg, making them suitable for small satellites and scientific missions. Medium-lift launch vehicles can transport payloads between 2,000 kg and 20,000 kg, adapting to various commercial and governmental needs. Heavy-lift launch vehicles are designed for missions requiring high-capacity payloads over 20,000 kg, essential for larger satellites and interplanetary missions.</p></p>
