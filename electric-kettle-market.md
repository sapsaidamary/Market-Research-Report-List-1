<p><p>The electric kettle market can be categorized into two main types: direct plug-in and rotation types. Direct plug-in kettles connect directly to a power source for heating, typically featuring a heating element at the base. In contrast, rotation-type kettles have a detachable base, allowing the kettle to be lifted and rotated for easy pouring without being restricted by a cord. Both types cater to different user preferences, influencing convenience and design in the electric kettle market.</p></p>
