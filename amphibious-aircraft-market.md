<p><p>The amphibious aircraft market is segmented into fixed-wing and rotary-wing categories. Fixed-wing amphibious aircraft feature wings for efficient flight and floats for water landings, making them suitable for transporting passengers and cargo. These craft excel in long-distance travel and can operate from both land and water. Rotary-wing amphibious aircraft, or seaplanes, utilize rotor systems for vertical takeoff and landing, providing versatility in confined spaces. They are ideal for search and rescue missions, agricultural applications, and accessing remote areas.</p></p>
