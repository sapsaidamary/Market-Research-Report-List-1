<p><p>The wired speaker market can be categorized into three main types based on frequency response: low frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency. Low-frequency speakers (subwoofers) enhance deep sounds, ideal for music genres like hip-hop and rock. Medium-frequency speakers handle vocals and instrumental sounds, providing clarity and richness for most audio content. High-frequency speakers (tweeters) reproduce treble sounds, adding brightness and detail to music. Each type plays a crucial role in delivering a balanced and immersive listening experience, catering to diverse audio preferences.</p></p>
