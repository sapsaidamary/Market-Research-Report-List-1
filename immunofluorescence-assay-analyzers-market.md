<p><ul><li>Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunofluorescence assay analyzers are essential tools in diagnostics, categorized into several market types. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) detects antibodies or antigens using enzyme-linked proteins. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) employs light emission for sensitive detection. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) utilizes radioactive isotopes for measuring substances. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) integrates enzyme and fluorescent detection, offering advantages in sensitivity and specificity. Each method caters to diverse analytical needs in clinical and research settings, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
