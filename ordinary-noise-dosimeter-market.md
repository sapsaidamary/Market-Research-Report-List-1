Cable dosimeters are connected to measuring equipment via cables, allowing for continuous noise monitoring in various environments. These systems often provide stable connectivity and power supply but can be limited by mobility. In contrast, cableless dosimeters use wireless technology for data transmission, offering greater portability and flexibility in monitoring noise levels in dynamic settings, making them ideal for personal use or challenging environments.</p></p>
