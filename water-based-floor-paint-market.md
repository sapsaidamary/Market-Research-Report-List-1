<p><ul><li>Water-Diluted Paint</li><li>Water Dispersible Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based floor paint market includes two main types: water-diluted paint and water-dispersible paint. Water-diluted paint is formulated to be mixed with water, enhancing ease of application and reducing volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-dispersible paint consists of resin particles dispersed in water, providing excellent adhesion and durability while being environmentally friendly. Both types serve various applications in residential and commercial settings, offering versatile solutions for flooring needs with user-friendly characteristics and lower health impacts.</p></p>
