<p><p>Portable torque testers are essential tools used to measure and calibrate torque in various applications. The market for these devices is divided into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic torque testers utilize compressed air for precise measurements, ideal for assembly lines. Hydraulic torque testers employ hydraulic pressure to provide accurate readings, often used in heavy machinery. Electric torque testers leverage electronic sensors for fast and reliable measurements, commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
