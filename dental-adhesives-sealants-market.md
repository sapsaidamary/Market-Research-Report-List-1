<p><p>Dental adhesives and sealants are vital for tooth restoration and protection. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a solvent, offering easy application and reduced toxicity, suitable for many dental procedures. Solvent-based adhesives, on the other hand, use organic solvents, which can enhance bonding strength but may present health hazards. Radiation-cured adhesives involve exposure to light or radiation to initiate curing, providing quick setting times and strong bonds. Each type caters to different dental needs, balancing efficacy with safety.</p></p>
