<p><p>The gas detection system market encompasses various technologies, each suited for specific applications. Semiconductor type sensors detect gases through resistance changes in materials. Electrochemical sensors operate via chemical reactions, ideal for measuring toxic gases. Catalytic combustion sensors are effective for flammable gases, using heat generation to signal detection. Photoionization detectors (PID) identify volatile organic compounds by ionizing gas molecules. Infrared sensors measure gas concentration based on absorption of infrared light, suitable for hydrocarbons and other gases, ensuring safety in various industries.</p></p>
