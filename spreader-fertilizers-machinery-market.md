<p><p>The spreader fertilizers machinery market comprises several types, including drop spreaders, rotary spreaders, and pendulum spreaders, along with other specialized options. Drop spreaders deliver precise amounts of fertilizer directly to the soil, minimizing waste and ensuring accuracy. Rotary spreaders use a spinning mechanism to distribute material over a wider area, suitable for larger spaces. Pendulum spreaders release fertilizer in a controlled arc, providing consistent coverage.
