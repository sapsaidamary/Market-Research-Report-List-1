<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are essential for safeguarding electrical systems against voltage spikes. The Voltage Switch Type SPD activates during over-voltage conditions, disconnecting the load to prevent damage. Pressure Limiting Type SPD regulates excess voltage by diverting energy, ensuring stable operation. Combination Type SPD integrates both functionalities, providing comprehensive protection by switching off the load and managing excess pressure. The market for these devices continues to grow due to increasing reliance on sensitive electronic equipment and the need for reliable power protection solutions.</p></p>
