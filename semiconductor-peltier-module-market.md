<p><p>The semiconductor Peltier module market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage modules. Single-stage modules consist of a single thermoelectric element, providing efficient cooling or heating for compact applications such as portable coolers and small electronic devices. In contrast, multi-stage modules incorporate multiple thermoelectric elements stacked together, enabling greater temperature differentials and enhanced cooling capabilities, suitable for applications like high-performance lasers and industrial equipment. The choice between them depends on specific cooling requirements and system design considerations.</p></p>
