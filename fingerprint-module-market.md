<p><p>The fingerprint module market encompasses three main types: Optical Fingerprint Modules use light to capture fingerprint images, offering high accuracy and ease of use. Silicon Fingerprint Modules utilize silicon-based sensors to detect fingerprint patterns, providing compact and efficient designs, often used in mobile devices. Capacitive Thin Film Transistor (TFT) Fingerprint Modules employ capacitive technology to read fingerprints by sensing electrical differences, enabling high-resolution scanning and integration into various devices, particularly in smartphones and security systems.</p></p>
