<p><strong>The Handheld Salt Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Type</li><li>Conductivity Type</li><li>Hydrometer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld salt meter market comprises three main types: optical type, conductivity type, and hydrometer type. The optical type uses light refraction to measure salt concentration, providing quick and non-destructive readings. The conductivity type measures the electrical conductivity of a solution, correlating it to salt concentration, making it suitable for various applications. The hydrometer type utilizes buoyancy principles to determine salt levels based on fluid density, offering a simple and low-cost solution for salt measurement in diverse environments.</p></p>
