<p><p>The extrication collar market is primarily divided into two types: hard collars and soft collars. Hard collars provide rigid support for the neck and spine, ensuring stability during patient transport and minimizing movement following traumatic injuries. They are typically more cumbersome but essential for severe cases. Soft collars offer flexible support, focusing on comfort and mild stabilization for less severe injuries. While hard collars are crucial in emergency situations, soft collars are often used for rehabilitation and chronic pain management.</p></p>
