<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Rubber (EPDM) is a synthetic elastomer known for its excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and weathering. The market is segmented into solution polymerization and suspension polymerization types. Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent to produce rubber with consistent properties and low viscosity, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, suspension polymerization occurs in an aqueous medium, resulting in a more stable product with different processing characteristics, suitable for various industrial uses and consumer products.</p></p>
