<p><ul><li>HTL Material</li><li>ETL Material</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED emissive layer materials market is categorized into three main types: Hole Transport Layer (HTL) materials, Electron Transport Layer (ETL) materials, and others. HTL materials facilitate the movement of positive holes towards the emissive layer, enhancing efficiency and brightness. ETL materials, on the other hand, support the transport of electrons, ensuring balanced charge injection. Other materials may include dopants and substrates that optimize overall device performance, stability, and color output, contributing to the advancement of OLED technology.</p></p>
