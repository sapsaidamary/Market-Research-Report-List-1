The Methomyl market can be categorized into two main types: WP (Wettable Powder) and EC (Emulsifiable Concentrate). WP formulations are dry powders that mix with water, providing effective pest control with minimal run-off. EC formulations, on the other hand, are liquid concentrates that emulsify in water, offering ease of application and penetration into plant tissues. Each type caters to different usage preferences and crop requirements.</p></p>
