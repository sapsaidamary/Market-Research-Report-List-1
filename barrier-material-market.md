PVDC is known for its excellent moisture and gas barrier properties, making it ideal for food packaging. EVOH offers superior oxygen barrier capabilities, enhancing shelf life for perishable goods. Other materials in this market include various polymers and composites tailored for specific packaging needs.</p></p>
