In contrast, automatic dispensers are designed for high-speed, high-volume applications, providing consistent and precise adhesive placement. These systems enhance efficiency and reduce labor costs, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
