<p><p>The medical tourniquet market is segmented into two primary types: latex tourniquets and TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are traditional, offering flexibility and a secure grip but may cause allergic reactions in some patients. TPE tourniquets, on the other hand, are made from synthetic materials that provide enhanced durability and are hypoallergenic, making them suitable for a broader range of patients. Both types are crucial in clinical settings for controlling bleeding during procedures and emergencies.</p></p>
