<p><p>Market pulp is categorized into different types based on fiber quality and application. BSK (Bleached Softwood Kraft) pulp is derived from softwood, offering high strength and is primarily used in producing high-quality papers. BHK (Bleached Hardwood Kraft) pulp comes from hardwood, providing good brightness and is suitable for various paper products. HYP (Hydrolyzed Pulp) is processed using hydrous methods, creating a pulp grade ideal for specialty applications like non-woven fabrics and tissue, focusing on softness and absorbency.</p></p>
