<p><p>A Base Transceiver Station (BTS) facilitates wireless communication by connecting mobile devices to a network. The market for BTS is divided into two main types: On-Grid and Off-Grid. On-Grid BTS relies on the electrical grid for power, suitable for urban areas with stable electricity supply. Off-Grid BTS, on the other hand, operates independently using renewable energy sources like solar or wind, making it ideal for rural or remote locations where grid access is limited or unreliable. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing mobile connectivity.</p></p>
