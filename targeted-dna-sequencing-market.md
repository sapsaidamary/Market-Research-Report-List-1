<p><p>The targeted DNA sequencing market comprises several key types, including exome sequencing, enrichment sequencing, amplicon sequencing, and others. Exome sequencing focuses on the protein-coding regions of the genome, capturing essential genetic information efficiently. Enrichment sequencing utilizes specific probes to isolate and analyze targeted genomic regions, enhancing detection sensitivity. Amplicon sequencing amplifies specific DNA segments for detailed exploration, ideal for studying mutations and variations. Other methods may include targeted resequencing and hybridization-based approaches, tailored for specific research or clinical needs.</p></p>
