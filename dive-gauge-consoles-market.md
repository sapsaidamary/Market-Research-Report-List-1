<p><p>Dive gauge consoles come in two main types: analog and digital. Digital gauges, on the other hand, feature electronic displays that provide real-time data, such as depth, time, and tank pressure, often enhanced with additional features like alarms and memory functions. Both types cater to diver preferences, balancing ease of use with the technological advancements in underwater monitoring.</p></p>
