<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads market consists of two primary types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for polishing harder materials, offering durability and fine surface finishing, crucial for applications like semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, soft CMP pads provide flexibility and conformability, making them ideal for softer materials and uneven surfaces. Both types play essential roles in enhancing surface quality in various industries, including electronics and optics, by facilitating precision polishing processes.</p></p>
