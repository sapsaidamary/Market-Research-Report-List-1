<p><p>The Abrasive Waterjet Cutting Machine market is categorized into two primary types: cantilever and gantry systems. Cantilever machines feature a single arm that supports the cutting head, allowing for efficient use of space and easy access for maintenance. These systems are typically more compact, suitable for smaller operations. In contrast, gantry machines utilize a rigid framework supported on tracks, enabling precise cutting over larger surfaces. They are ideal for high-volume production and can handle larger materials, offering greater versatility.</p></p>
