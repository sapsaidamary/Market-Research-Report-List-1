<p><p>Antiscalants are chemical compounds used to prevent the formation and deposition of scale in water treatment systems, particularly in reverse osmosis (RO), nanofiltration, and other membrane-based processes. They work by inhibiting the crystallization of minerals such as calcium carbonate, calcium sulfate, and barium sulfate, which can clog membranes and reduce system efficiency. Antiscalants are widely used in industries such as power generation, oil and gas, desalination, and food and beverage.</p><p>The Antiscalant Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for clean water, rising industrialization, and the expansion of desalination projects worldwide. Overall, the antiscalant market is poised for steady growth, driven by technological innovations and the global emphasis on sustainable water management.</p></p>
