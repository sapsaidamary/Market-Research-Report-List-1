<p><p>Polyurethane foam insulation is categorized into three main types: flexible foam, spray foam, and rigid foam. Flexible foam is commonly used in furniture and bedding, offering comfort and cushioning while providing decent insulation. Spray foam, applied as a liquid that expands upon contact, is ideal for sealing gaps and enhancing energy efficiency in buildings. Rigid foam, known for its high insulation value, is used in walls, roofs, and floors, providing structural support and superior thermal performance.</p></p>
