<p><strong>The Shared Web Hosting Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IP-based</li><li>Name-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shared web hosting services can be categorized into two main types: IP-based and name-based hosting. In IP-based hosting, each website on the server has a unique IP address, allowing for better isolation and security. This setup is often preferred by websites requiring SSL certificates. Conversely, name-based hosting shares a single IP address among multiple websites, distinguishing them through their domain names. It's a cost-effective solution suitable for smaller websites, though it may present limitations in terms of security and performance.</p></p>
