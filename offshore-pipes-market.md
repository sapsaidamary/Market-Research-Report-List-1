<p><strong>The Offshore Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore pipes market includes various types tailored for subsea applications. ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes are known for their cost-effectiveness and suitability for low-pressure pipelines. LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes provide high strength and are ideal for large diameter and high-pressure applications. SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes are recognized for their flexibility and are used in diverse offshore projects due to their ability to withstand harsh conditions. Each type has unique attributes tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
