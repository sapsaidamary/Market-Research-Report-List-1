<p><p>The industrial bioprocessing market is divided into upstream and downstream bioprocessing. Upstream bioprocessing involves the initial phases of production, including cell cultivation, fermentation, and bioreactor management, focusing on maximizing the yield of desired products like proteins or enzymes. Conversely, downstream bioprocessing encompasses the purification and recovery of these products, involving separation techniques, filtration, and refining processes to ensure product quality and safety. Together, these segments drive the efficiency and effectiveness of biotechnological production in various industries.</p></p>
