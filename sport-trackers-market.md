<p><p>Sport trackers are categorized into three main market types: wrist-based, chest strap, and others. Wrist-based trackers, like smartwatches and fitness bands, offer convenience and continuous monitoring of heart rate, activity levels, and sleep. Chest straps, typically used by serious athletes, provide accurate heart rate data through sensors placed on the chest. The "others" category includes various devices such as clip-on trackers, smart clothing, and portable fitness devices that serve specific needs or preferences in tracking physical activity and health metrics.</p></p>
