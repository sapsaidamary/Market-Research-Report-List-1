<p><p>A Pulse Oximetry Probe is a medical device used to measure the oxygen saturation levels in a patient's blood non-invasively. It typically consists of a light-emitting diode (LED) and a photodetector, which assess the absorption of light by hemoglobin to determine oxygen saturation. These probes are crucial in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, and home health environments, enabling healthcare providers to monitor patients’ respiratory and cardiac conditions effectively.</p><p>The Pulse Oximetry Probe Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period.
