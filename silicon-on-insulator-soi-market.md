<p><p>Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) technology is a semiconductor fabrication technique that uses a layered structure of silicon, an insulator (typically silicon dioxide), and another layer of silicon. This configuration improves electrical performance, reduces parasitic capacitance, and enhances device scaling, making SOI ideal for high-performance and low-power applications in electronics.</p><p>The SOI market is witnessing significant growth, propelled by the increasing demand for energy-efficient devices, particularly in the semiconductor and consumer electronics sectors. The rise of advanced technologies such as the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), and 5G wireless communication is further driving the need for SOI solutions, which offer superior performance and reduced power consumption.</p><p>Key trends shaping the SOI market include the development of fully depleted SOI (FD-SOI) technology, which enhances performance and allows for lower voltage operation, and the growing adoption of SOI in automotive and industrial applications due to its reliability and efficiency. The Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period, highlighting its critical role in the ongoing evolution of the semiconductor industry and the broader electronics market.</p></p>
