<p><p>Copper azole wood preservatives are categorized into three types: CBA-A, CA-B, and CA-C. CBA-A is a water-based formulation primarily designed for above-ground applications, offering protection against fungi and termites. CA-B provides enhanced protection for ground-contact applications, ensuring durability and resistance to decay. CA-C is specifically tailored for more demanding environments, suitable for heavy-duty applications like structural lumber, providing superior long-term protection. Each type is formulated to meet specific performance standards and application requirements in the wood treatment market.</p></p>
