<p><p>The TIG welding torch market comprises two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled torches. Water-cooled torches are designed for high-performance applications, efficiently dissipating heat through circulating water, making them ideal for extended use and heavy-duty welding tasks. In contrast, air-cooled torches rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more portable and suitable for lighter applications. Both types cater to different user needs, balancing factors like cooling efficiency, portability, and welding intensity, influencing their respective market demands.</p></p>
