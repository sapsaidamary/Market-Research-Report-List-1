<p><strong>The Digital X-Ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital X-Ray Systems market is primarily categorized into Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR) technologies. CR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems use imaging plates to capture data, which is then converted into digital images through a processing system, offering flexibility and lower costs. In contrast, DR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems utilize flat-panel detectors for immediate digital image acquisition, providing higher image quality, faster processing times, and enhanced workflow efficiency, making them ideal for high-volume medical environments.</p></p>
