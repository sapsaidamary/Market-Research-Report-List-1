<p><p>Valve markets encompass various types tailored for specific functions. Shut-off valves are designed to completely stop or allow flow in pipelines. Control valves regulate the flow of liquids or gases by adjusting the opening based on variable conditions. Vent valves release excess pressure or allow air to enter systems, ensuring safe operation. Diverter valves redirect flow between different paths in a system. Other valves include safety, check, and pressure relief valves, serving diverse industrial needs and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
