<p><p>The dental mouth openers market primarily includes two types: mouth props and mouth gags. Mouth props are designed to keep the mouth open comfortably during dental procedures, allowing for better access and visibility. They are typically used in routine check-ups and treatments. Mouth gags, on the other hand, are more rigid instruments that secure the mouth in an open position, often used in surgeries or extensive procedures where limiting movement is essential. Both types enhance efficiency and patient safety in dental care.</p></p>
