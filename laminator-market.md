<p><strong>The Laminator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pouch Laminator</li><li>Roll Laminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminator market comprises two primary types: pouch laminators and roll laminators. Pouch laminators are designed for individual documents, utilizing pre-sized pouches that encase the item for a protective finish, ideal for home or small office use. In contrast, roll laminators use large rolls of laminating film, allowing for higher volume and continuous lamination for larger prints or multiple documents, making them suitable for commercial applications such as print shops and schools. Both types enhance durability and presentation.</p></p>
