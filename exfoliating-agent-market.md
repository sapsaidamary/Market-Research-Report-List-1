<p><p>The exfoliating agent market is primarily divided into physical and chemical exfoliating agents. In contrast, chemical exfoliating agents utilize acids or enzymes to dissolve bonds between skin cells, allowing for deeper penetration and improved skin rejuvenation. Both types cater to various skin concerns, offering options for consumers seeking to achieve smoother, brighter, and healthier complexions.</p></p>
