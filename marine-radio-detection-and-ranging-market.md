<p><strong>The Marine Radio Detection And Ranging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X Band Radars</li><li>S Band Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Radio Detection and Ranging (RADAR) market primarily consists of two main types: X-band radars and S-band radars. X-band radars, operating at higher frequencies, provide high-resolution images, making them ideal for navigation, surveillance, and target detection in coastal and offshore environments. In contrast, S-band radars, functioning at lower frequencies, offer longer range capabilities and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making them suitable for tracking larger vessels and monitoring weather patterns. Both types are essential for maritime safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
