<p><p>The HVAC contactor market is categorized into various types based on the number of poles: 1-pole, 2-pole, 3-pole, and 4-pole types. </p><p>1-Pole contactors are used for single-phase systems, while 2-Pole options support two-phase applications. 3-Pole contactors are ideal for three-phase systems, commonly found in industrial settings. 4-Pole contactors cater to three-phase loads with an additional pole for neutral connections, ensuring enhanced control and safety. Each type serves specific application needs within heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
