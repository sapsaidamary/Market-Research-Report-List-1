<p><p>The pulse picking system market encompasses technologies that selectively extract ultrafast pulses from continuous wave lasers or laser systems. Key types include electro-optic modulators (EOMs), which utilize electric fields to control light properties for precise pulse generation, and acousto-optic modulators (AOMs), which employ sound waves to diffract and modulate light. Both technologies are crucial for applications in telecommunications, scientific research, and industrial processes, enabling high-speed data transmission and advanced laser experiments by providing tailored pulse timing and duration.</p></p>
