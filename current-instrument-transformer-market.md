<p><ul><li>Dry Type Current Transformer</li><li>Pouring Type Current Transformer</li><li>Oil-Immersed Current Transformer</li><li>Gas Insulated Current Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current instrument transformer market features several types, each designed for specific applications. Dry type current transformers utilize solid insulation, offering high reliability and low maintenance. Pouring type transformers use resin for insulation, ensuring moisture resistance and stability. Oil-immersed transformers are filled with insulating oil, providing excellent thermal management and electrical insulation. Gas insulated current transformers are compact and use gas as an insulating medium, making them ideal for space-constrained environments and high-voltage applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
