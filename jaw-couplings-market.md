<p><p>Jaw couplings are mechanical devices designed to transmit torque and rotational motion between two shafts while accommodating misalignment. They consist of two jaws (usually made from materials like aluminum or plastic) that connect with a flexible elastomeric element in the center, ensuring effective torque transmission while minimizing shock loads. Known for their simplicity and effectiveness, jaw couplings are widely used in various applications, including manufacturing, robotics, and conveyor systems.</p><p>The Jaw Couplings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for high-performance machinery across diverse industries.
