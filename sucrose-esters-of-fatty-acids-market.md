<p><strong>The Sucrose Esters of Fatty Acids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High HLB (Above 9)</li><li>Medium HLB (7-9)</li><li>Low HLB (Below 6)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sucrose esters of fatty acids are categorized by their hydrophilic-lipophilic balance (HLB) values, which determine their applications in various industries. High HLB (above 9) esters are effective emulsifiers for oil-in-water formulations, commonly used in food and cosmetic products. Medium HLB (7-9) esters serve dual functions, balancing oil and water phases in creams and lotions. Low HLB (below 6) esters stabilize water-in-oil emulsions, making them ideal for products like margarine and certain lubricants. Each type plays a significant role in enhancing texture and stability.</p></p>
