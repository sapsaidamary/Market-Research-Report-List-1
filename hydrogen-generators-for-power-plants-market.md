<p><strong>The Hydrogen Generators for Power Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrogen generators for power plants primarily utilize two types of electrolysis technologies: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use a liquid alkaline electrolyte to separate hydrogen and oxygen, offering a cost-effective solution with established technology. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, providing higher efficiency and operational flexibility, especially under varying loads. Both technologies play critical roles in the transition toward cleaner energy by producing hydrogen for various applications, including fuel cells and industrial processes.</p></p>
