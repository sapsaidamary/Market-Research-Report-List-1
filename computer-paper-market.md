<p><ul><li>Non-Perforated</li><li>Perforated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer paper market is divided into non-perforated and perforated types. Non-perforated paper is solid, providing a continuous feed for printers, ideal for bulk printing tasks. It is commonly used for invoices and reports. In contrast, perforated paper features pre-cut lines, allowing easy separation of individual sheets or forms. This type is popular for applications where documents need to be detached, such as tickets or receipts. Each type serves distinct purposes based on printing needs and document handling.</p></p>
