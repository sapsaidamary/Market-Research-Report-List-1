<p><p>The composites consumables market comprises various types, including films, peel plies, flow nettings, and surfacing veils. Films serve as protective barriers and improve surface finish, while peel plies facilitate easy release of molds or layups. Flow nettings enhance resin distribution during infusion processes, ensuring uniform material wet-out. Surfacing veils function to protect against environmental damage and improve aesthetic quality. Together, these consumables play crucial roles in the manufacturing and performance of composite materials, particularly in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
