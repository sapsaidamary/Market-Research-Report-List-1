<p><strong>The Foam-based Extinguishing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Expansion Foam-based Extinguishing System</li><li>Medium Expansion Foam-based Extinguishing System</li><li>High Expansion Foam-based Extinguishing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam-based extinguishing system market is categorized into three main types: low expansion, medium expansion, and high expansion foam systems. Low expansion systems produce foam with a slightly turbulent structure, suitable for firefighting in flammable liquid scenarios. Medium expansion foam systems generate denser foam for both liquid and solid fires, providing superior coverage and stability. High expansion foam systems create a lightweight and vast foam blanket, ideal for large spaces, effectively smothering fires and minimizing vapor release in industrial settings.</p></p>
