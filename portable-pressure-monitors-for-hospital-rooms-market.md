<p><p>Portable pressure monitors in hospital rooms are essential for maintaining optimal airflow and ensuring patient safety. Negative sensor monitoring detects pressure variations that may indicate air leaks or inadequate ventilation, thus preventing contamination. Differential sensor monitoring measures pressure differences between two spaces, ensuring that critical areas remain sterile and controlled. Both types of monitoring systems enhance infection control, improve patient care, and support compliance with healthcare regulations by providing real-time data on environmental conditions within hospital settings.</p></p>
