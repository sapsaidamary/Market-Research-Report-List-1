<p><p>The photovoltaics modules market comprises three primary types: single crystal silicon, polycrystalline silicon, and others. Single crystal silicon modules, known for their high efficiency and longevity, are made from pure silicon crystals. Polycrystalline silicon modules, while slightly less efficient, are more cost-effective and have a simpler manufacturing process. The "others" category includes thin-film technologies like cadmium telluride and organic photovoltaics, offering flexibility and lightweight options suitable for various applications, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
